About Me

Welcome to my online work portfolio.
I provide strategy and solutions in the form of web presence.

Who am I?

I am an ambitious programmer out to create successful applications and websites in the competitive modern web. Ever the entrepreneur, new ideas and innovation greatly captures my attention and propels me to research and be on top of upcoming technologies all the time. As a Computer Science major from Vancouver, BC Canada, my development career began as a part-time web developer for a local company, IntelliDT, during my undergraduate studies.

My Career

Since then, my web development abilities took off and have spread to a variety of professional networks. I now provide freelance web services to multiple clients in many industries ranging from retail stores to auto-services to startup technology companies. I have even pursued an entrepreneurial partnership and career as a Lead Developer for Procurify.com, a cloud enterprise SaaS startup company.

Methodology

What makes me different from other developers? I care about your ideas. I care about your goals and I care about using the latest beneficial technology to keep your vision relevant on the web. I care about user experience and I care about what your web site/application's users pay attention to. Your success is very important to me, because I enjoy building success!
